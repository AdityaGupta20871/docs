# Invoices & Usages

## Invoices
You can find the invoices for all your purchases in the Invoices section under the Billing tab. \
Select the durations to see their respective transaction details.

## Usages
You can check your current plan usage in the Plan Usage section under the Billing tab. \
It features a visual representation of all the services included in your plan.
