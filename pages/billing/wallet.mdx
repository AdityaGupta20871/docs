# Connect Wallet

## For All Networks except Solana

Follow these steps to connect your wallet:

1. Go to [Plan](https://app.spheron.network/#/app/billing/plan) under the Billing tab and Click Connect Wallet.
2. Choose your desired network (except Solana), token, and wallet, and Click Save.
3. Sign the transaction in your selected wallet to connect your wallet.

## For Solana

Follow these steps to connect your wallet:

1. Go to [Plan](https://app.spheron.network/#/app/billing/plan) under the Billing tab and Click Connect Wallet.
2. Choose Solana as your network, SOL as your token, and Phantom as your wallet, and Click Save.
3. Sign the transaction in Phantom to connect your wallet.
