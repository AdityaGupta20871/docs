# Organization Settings

- **General:** You can update the details of your organization here.
- **Archived Projects:** You can find all the archived projects here. To unarchive a project, go to its settings and Click Unarchive.

## Archive Project
Follow these steps to archive your project:
1. Select the deployed project from the organization dashboard that you wish to archive.
2. Go to General under the Settings tab.
3. Scroll down to the Archive Project section.
4. Check the checkbox to confirm and Click Archive.
